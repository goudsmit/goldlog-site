Notes for Olympia Desvall
New York Times, dd 3.9.1907: “With several circus features added to its acts of last year, the Hippodrome has begun its third season. ........ Olympia Desvall, a graceful rider with a troup of well-trained dogs are among the new features for the Fall season....”
1914 “Madame Olympia Desvall in Barnum & Bailey Greatest Show on Earth” on a lithograph colour poster printed by Strobridge, Cincinnati.
Notes for Julius b Joseph (Spouse 1)
16.3.1914 moving to USA, on ship Pretoria from Hamburg: Gaudesmith-Desvall, Julius (42), Olympia (37) with children: Josef (18), Maximilian (16), Emanuel (11); last permanent residence Amsterdam, nearest relative m.i.l. Elise Blumenfeld, Holland Amsterdam; joining Barnum & Baily Circus.
[NB: though in the ship manifest his birthplace was explicitly stated as Tilburg, Holland, there is no birthact of him in Tilburg; but his father was born in Tilburg]
