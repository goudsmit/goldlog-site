[NB: notifier in his death act is his foster-son Benjamin Levi Hijmans (33) stated as his “neeff” (= cousin!), which might mean that Benjamin’s father Hijman Levi was another brother of Jonas. But there is no further proof for this and the word cousin is sometimes used for kinship in a general meaning]
