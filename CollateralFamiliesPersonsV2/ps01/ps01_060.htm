Notes for Jacob b Samuel (Spouse 1)
[Rather unusual to choose a surname differing from his 2 older brothers: Goudsmit & Bloemhof. Anyway, Jacob Samuel didn’t assume a (new) surname in 1811. In acts from 1814 and 1815 he’s still named Jacob Levy. Only in his death act he is stated with the double surname Levy Groenewoud -- maybe because meanwhile his son Samuel had elsewhere registered the new name Groenewoudt. When and where is so far not found. His daughter Eva is found for the first time stated with the name Groenewoud in a notarial act dd 1820 in Tholen]
