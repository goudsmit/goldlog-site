Notes for Jeannette b Salomon Speyer
oldest sister of a.o: # Moses (born abt 1840?), who married (abt 1876, Frankfurt) to Lina b NN; they had at least 2 sons Saly/Salomon and Edmund, who were living together in Frankfurt, good friends of Hans Eliazar Goudsmit (b. 1904). # Salomine (born abt 1840?) , who married (abt 1872, Cleve) to her cousin Leopold Speyer from Holland. # Jettchen (born abt 1840?), who had an ailing lung illness and died young. # Frona (born abt 1840?), who was living nearby in Krefeld.
