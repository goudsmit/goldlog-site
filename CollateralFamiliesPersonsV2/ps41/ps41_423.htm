Notes for Philip b Marcus van Son
son of Mordechai b Simcha -- sometimes spelled “van Zon”.
[NB: many contradicting data about his parents. In his Death Act his parents were stated as Samuel Philip and Fronika Merkes, but it is more likely those were the parents of his former (first?) wife, who was stated as Marianna Mozes; his surviving widow was stated as Hendriena Isaac]
[NB: there was another Philip Marcus van Son (b. 1773 Bronkhorst; d. 19.1.1850 London, England]
Notes for Maria b Samuel (Spouse 1)

# Marigje Simons (in marriage act of Matje, Puttershoek), but also Maria Samuel!
