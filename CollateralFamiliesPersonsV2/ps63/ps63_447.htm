Notes for Fija b Samuel Cohen
later stated as Sija Hartog, in the marriage act of her son Salomon dd 1839, where she was mentioned as deceased, though her Death-act was not enclosed in the HuwBijl. This contradicts the possibility that she might be the same person as Elias’ second Trijntje Samuel Harst, who died in 1843. She is not mentioned in the death act of her husband (1832).
