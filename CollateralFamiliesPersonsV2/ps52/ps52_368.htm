Notes for Meijer b Hartog Groen (Groenteman)
[NB, death act: “born alhier (= Amsterdam), weduwnaar van Marianne Manes Kinsbergen verder niets bekend” (parents not known)]
[NB: both his daughters are namend Judik!!]
[Allthough in his death act his birthplace is stated as Amsterdam (”alhier”) it’s highly probable that he is a brother of Mozes Hartog Groenteman (b. abt 1740 in Germany; d 20.6.1826 Amsterdam, age 95!?) and Philip Hartog Groenluij/Groenman (b. 1765 Manheim; d 27.12.1832, age 72). The last one was married in 1792 (witness: “brother of the groom Meijer Hartog”!) to Marina Barend Bon (b. 1770 Amsterdam) daughter of Barend Simon Bon & Jannetje Marcus Kalker (d. 12.9.1827 Amsterdam, age 87) -- cf 5. Furthermore it’s remarkable that the last one’s surname “Kalker” is the same as the at first incorrectly stated surname of Meijer’s daughter-in-law: Leentje Levi Kalker (instead of Koekoek!)-- cf her note. Another remarkable fact: his great-granddaughter Leentje Salomon Groen’s mother-in-law also has the surname “Kalker”; Magdalena Salomon Kalker (b. 1811 Geertruidenberg)]
[NB; he may also be a brother of Hendrik Hartog de Groen (= Hartog Hars Groen) who died on 30.12.1821 (age 75, born in Germany; elsewhere his birth placce was stated as Amsterdam) In 1812 he had 5 sons and 4 daughters.]
