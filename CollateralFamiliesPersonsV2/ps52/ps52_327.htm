Notes for Lion b Hermanus (Samuel) Kinsbergen
20.1.1812 Amsterdam, NaamsAanneming act (B, 105) by Lion Kinsbergen, living Leprozengracht No -; he keeps the surname Kinsbergen; he has 3 sons, 3 daughters, 5 grandsons and 2 granddaughters. Son Abraham (32), living Weesperveld, married to Saara Mosseis (with children) Daniel (8), Samuel (5), Monna (4), Betje (2). Son Jochem (31), living Amstelveld married to Neeltje Schols (with children) Samuel (6), Manes (4), Willem (2). Son Markus (22). Daughters Grietje (29), Elisabeth (27), Julia (25). his signature “Leon Kinsbergen”
1798 Amsterdam, Leon Kinsbergen takes over the Manege and Ropewalking Group from Pieter Magito; it is the beginning of a 2 century long Circus tradition.

zijn discipelen te exerceeren in het voltigeeren. ....” (uit 8ste Jaarboek vereniging Amstellodamum)]
