15.1.1910 arriving in USA, on ship The Lusitania from Liverpool: Arnold Goudsmidt (41y 8m) impressario, René (19y 5m) musical student, John Adalbert (16y 1m), musical student; Nationality Holland, last residence Kopenhagen; passage was paid by his brother (name not mentioned) -- the ages of the cildren are not correct. -- Strangely they are also stated in the ship manifest of The Campania from Liverpool that arrived 23.1.1910 (= 8 days later!), here spelled Goudschmidt, the same incorrect ages.
23.12.1921 arriving in USA (permanent), on ship The Adriatic from Southampton: Arnold Goudsmidt (53) actor, Mari Anna Goudsmidt (51), wife, and children Adolf (16) artist, Arnolda (20) artist, Henning (14) artist; Nationality Dutch, last residence Amsterdam; joining P. Tausig NY.
