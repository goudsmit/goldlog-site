but in the HuwelijksBijlagen of his first marriage act is included an extract from Amsterdam stating the death of Jochem Levy Kinsbergen dd 5.9.1835, without mentioning the place; and also an extract stating the circumcision of Samuel Jochem on 18.2.1828 -- the differing names of both father Jacob/Jochem and son Hermanus/Samuel are approved in a special notarial declaration!]
