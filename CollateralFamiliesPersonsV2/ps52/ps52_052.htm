[IMDb website, quote: ‘The Gaudsmith Brothers were vaudevillians Max and Henry Gaudsmith, who did a poodle act. They were born in The Netherlands and migrated to the USA in the first part of the twentieth century. Max was born circa 1880, date of death not known. Henry was born 20 November 1880 and died in Florida 24 October 1980” -- NB: these given birth years (1880) seem improbable, as the Gaudsmith Brothers were still performing on Television in 1961 (Perry Como Show).
