Notes for Maartje-Sally b Salomon van Witsen
Maartje’s mother Betje was a sister of Rachel Emanuel Simons van Noorden, mother of Mietje Eliazar Denekamp, who was married to Jonas Mozes Goudsmit, the oldest (step)brother of Maartje’s spouse Jacob Wiener.
Notes for Jacob b Samuel (Spouse 1)
When Jacob was 8 month old his father died. He became the (step) son of his mothers second spouse, Mozes Jonas Goudsmit, who also was a cousin of his father (= nephew of his grandmother Rebecca).
