Notes for Eva b Salomon Simons van Noorden
stated in her death act as “fille de Salomon Simon & Rachel Emanuel”
[NB: stated in Marriage Registration Amsterdam dd 20.4.1792 as “Eva Salomon Siemons van Nimwegen Jodin oud 25 jaar in de Rapenburgerstraat geadst. met consent van haar moeder Rachel Emanuel” -- both she and her husband were mentioned as 25 years old, but according to several other statements they must have been considerably older!]
1812 dying in s-Gravenhage, sexion S.340
[NB: In some genealogies5 she is considered a daughter of Salomon (Zalman!) Simon van Norden (b. 1743 Amsterdam d. 26.1.1807 Amsterdam buried Muiderberg) who married 1762 Amsterdam to Telsje Mozes Monk (b. 1740 Amsterdam d. 20.9.1807 Amsterdam buried (as widow of Zalman) in Muiderberg) -- this is not correct because Eva’s father Salomon was named in hebrew Zelig, not Zalman. Furthermore her real mother Rachel died in 1810 in s-Gravenhage and was buried there]
