Notes for Grietje/Judik b Levi (Spouse 1)
daughter of Levie Salomon Stokvis & Jacomina Abraham Cohen -- also stated as Margaretha Stokvisch
[NB: contradictions about her birth date -- in the act of her second marriage she is stated as “widow of Mozes Marcus Goudsmit, born in the month March 1783 in Rotterdam”, and also by deduction from her age at death she would have been born in 1783 (in Amsterdam!). So she can hardly be the mother of Basje (Betje) Goudsmit, who was born in 1793 (deduced from the age in her death act) or in 1795 (deduced from her age 16 at Naamsaanneming in 1811). Elsewhere (in Akevoth 5) her birth date is stated as 1771 (i.e. more than 12 years older) Conclusion: The Notoriety act with her second marriage is very wrong in many aspects or there are two spouses, both named Grietje or Judik Stokvis, both daughter of Levie Salomon & Jacomina Abraham, the first one having died shortly before 1800; which would explain the naming of daughter Judic (b 1800) and that two daughters have been named Betje -- needs further research]
