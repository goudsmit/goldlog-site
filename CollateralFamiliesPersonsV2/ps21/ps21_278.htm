Notes for Maria b Eliazar (?) Danielson
All facts have to be checked further!
In her death act (17.8.1839, Rotterdam) her father is stated as Daniel Danielson and her mother as Judik Boas and her birthplace as Rotterdam. This can’t be all right.
Most plausible is that Maria, not yet 2 years old, became an orphan, because her mother Judik Samuel Boas deceased within days after her birth and her father Eliazar Samuel died in january 1802; and that, like her brother Samuel Eliazar, she had become a foster child of Daniel Danielson, who was married to her mother’s older sister Golda Samuel Boas.
