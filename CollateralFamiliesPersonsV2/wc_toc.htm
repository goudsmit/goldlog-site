Introduction
Goudsmit Segal & Collateral Families

A never ending string -- linking over 30000 parents and children of jewish families, who were dwelling mainly in the Rotterdam Region and in the Rhine Delta -- i.e. on the Islands of Zuid-Holland and Zeeland and in the provinces NoordBrabant and Gelderland. All - by parentage, sibship or marriage - related to Jonas Samuel Goudsmit Segal (1739-1817)
