Family of Jacob b Samuel Levi Groenewoudt (5) & Lena b Andries Lion

28. Samuel b Jacob Levi (Groenewoudt). Born in 1778 in Steenbergen. died on 24.4.1842 in Oirschot. Occupation: Vleeschhouwer (1840).

Samuel b Jacob married Maria-Anna b Salomon Roost (Roos), daughter of Salomon b David Roost (1749-9.2.1839) & Clara b Alexander (abt 1750-). Born on 6.6.1775 in Oirschot. died on 24.1.1852 in Oirschot.

[NB: in her death act she is stated as Marian de Roos]

29. Eva b Jacob Levi (Groenewoudt). Born on 14.10.1787 in Steenbergen. died on 30.6.1823 in Tholen.
