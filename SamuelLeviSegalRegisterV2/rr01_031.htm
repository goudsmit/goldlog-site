Family of Eva b Jacob Wiener (Benjamin Wiener) (34) & David b Mozes Klein

145. Mozes b David Klein. Born on 19.3.1824 in Aarle-Rixtel. Occupation: Slagter.

also stated as Mozes David Klijn
22.3.1869 moving from Bergeijk to Valkenswaard (with his wife and 3 children; and his mother and his brother Samuel)
9.12.1876 moving from Valkenswaard to Roermond (with 2nd wife and children and brother Samuel)
21.4.1877 moving from Roermond to Woensel (with his family and brother Samuel)
16.5.1879 moving from Woensel to Valkenswaard (with his famly and brother Samuel)
11.6.1884 moving from Valkenswaard back to Woensel (with family and brother Samuel)
5.9.1890 moving from Woensel to Stratum (with family (not all children) and brother Samuel)

On 27.10.1862 when Mozes b David was 38, he first married Judith b Mozes Hertog in Susteren. Born on 9.2.1830 in Linne. died on 2.1.1871 in Valkenswaard.

daughter of Mousus Hertog & Mina Kroonenberg

They had the following children:
521 i. David-Herman b Mozes Klein (17.9.1863-)
522 ii. Louisa-Helena b Mozes Klein (1.7.1865-)
523 iii. Alexander-Samuel b Mozes Klein (25.3.1868-16.4.1943)

On 19.7.1872 when Mozes b David was 48, he second married Adelheid b Salomon Zanders in Valkenswaard. Born on 7.4.1837 in Germany, Bracht (near Brüggen).

daughter of Salomon Zanders & Veronika Schnucks
1872 living in Sittard

They had one child:
524 i. Henriette b Mozes Klein (13.7.1879-15.10.1942)
