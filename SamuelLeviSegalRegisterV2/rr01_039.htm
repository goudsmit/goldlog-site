191. Lena b Samuel Goudsmit. Born on 23.1.1846 in Oud-Beijerland. died on 4.7.1918 in Rotterdam.

1908 living in Gouda

On 6.12.1865 when Lena b Samuel was 19, she married Meijer b Mozes van Driel, son of Mozes b Levi van Driel (1785-23.4.1851) & Catherina/Kaatje b Meijer van den Meijdenberg (1796-29.11.1872), in Aarlanderveen. Born in 1834 in Driel. died on 16.10.1890 in Driel.

They had one child:
i. Kaatje b Meijer van Driel (1867-)

192. Abraham b Samuel Goudsmit. Born on 26.12.1848 in Alphen ad Rijn. died aft 1885 in USA?

9.9.1885 his family moves from Aarlanderveen: Abraham is in the USA, no further trace

193. Simon b Samuel Goudsmit. Born on 25.9.1851 in Alphen ad Rijn. died aft 1911 in Belgium, Antwerpen? Occupation: Agent Commerciale (1908).

7.6.1902 moving with family from Loosduinen to Naaldwijk,
1.11.1904 moving with family from Maassluis, Havenstraat to Belgium
several addresses: Schaerbeek, Auderghhem, Brussel, Oostende, Berchem
29.5.1908 to Antwerpen, Rue du Vanneau 136 (Police Immigration 125854)
27.6.1911 moving with his family from Borgerhout back to Antwerpen, Ketsstraat 13 (also file 12854)

On 29.4.1885 when Simon b Samuel was 33, he married Alida b Hartog Bosman, daughter of Hartog Eliazar b Hendrik Bosman (18.6.1829-bef 1885) & Sina b Simon de Haas (21.8.1826-aft 1885), in s-Gravenhage. Born on 27.6.1855 in s-Gravenhage. died aft 1911 in Belgium, Antwerpen?

They had the following children:
i. Sylvain-Henri b Simon Samuel Goudsmit (No Offspring) (10.1.1887-25.1.1943)
ii. Francina Louisa b Simon Samuel Goudsmit (Investigate) (11.6.1896-? )

194. Levi b Samuel Goudsmit. Born on 16.2.1857 in Aarlanderveen. died aft 1893 in Amsterdam? Occupation: Huidenzouter (1886).

1878 moving to Amsterdam, living on several addresses with his nephew Simon Hartog Goudsmit. is unmarried. Since 16.12.1893 off the track.
1886 Living Amsterdam, Prinsengracht 772
