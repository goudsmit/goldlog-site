32. Samuel b Jacob Wiener (Benjamin Wiener). Born on 4.4.1788 in Bergeijk. died on 11.4.1826 in Oud-Beijerland. Occupation: Koopman (1823).

On 30.11.1823 when Samuel b Jacob was 35, he married Belia b Salomon Sluis (Sluijs), daughter of Salomon b Marcus Sluis (Sluijs) (9.7.1767-6.6.1812) & Rebekka b David van Krevel (van Creefeld) (1767-7.4.1853), in Oud-Beijerland. Born on 13.12.1799 in Dordrecht. died on 28.1.1845 in Oud-Beijerland. Occupation: Dienstmeid (1823), Koopvrouw (1827).

1899 born in Dordrecht, in de Raamstraat (acc to Notoriety act)
