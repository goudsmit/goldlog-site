Family of Joseph b David Klein (148) & Cornelia b Herman Kaufman

525. David b Joseph Klein. Born on 23.11.1868 in Bergeijk. died on 27.11.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

On 24.8.1904 when David b Joseph was 35, he married Sientje b Israel Bromet in Arnhem. Born on 25.9.1871 in Arnhem. died on 27.11.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

daughter of Israel Salomon Bromet & Eva Bachrach

526. Herman b Joseph Klein. Born on 31.7.1870 in Tilburg. died on 23.11.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim). Occupation: Koopman (1906).

1906 moving from Antwerpen to Stratum to Tilburg (in the same year acc to BR Stratum)

On 13.1.1901 when Herman b Joseph was 30, he married Mina b Hartog Hollander, daughter of Herrie/Hartog Abraham b Juda Hollander (14.1.1844-15.3.1908) & Rozette b David Cohen (8.2.1849-7.6.1878), in Tilburg. Born on 15.3.1877 in Venlo. died on 14.5.1943 in k Sobibor.

They had the following children:
i. Josina b Herman Klein (30.12.1899-? )
ii. Rosetta b Herman Klein (6.11.1903-9.4.1943)
iii. Cornelia b Herman Klein (4.10.1905-6.3.1944)
iv. Joseph b Herman Klein (23.11.1908-30.4.1943)

527. Eva b Joseph Klein. Born on 25.8.1872 in Tilburg.
