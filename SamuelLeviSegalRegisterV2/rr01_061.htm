Family of Saartje b Salomon Mendels (81) & Juda b Hartog Monnikendam

339. Salomon b Juda Monnikendam. Born on 20.9.1863 in Brielle. Occupation: Arts, Officier v Gezondheid Indische leger.

He was married to (1) Teuntje Elena Leonie Loze and (2) Anna Elbertha van Hilten

340. Hartog b Juda Monnikendam. Born on 13.6.1865 in Brielle. died on 23.5.1943 in k Sobibor. Occupation: Onderwijzer (1894).

also spelled Munnikendam
He was second married to Wilhelmina Adele Franco (from Amsterdam)

On 31.1.1894 when Hartog b Juda was 28, he married Bruintje b Jonas Mendels (328) , daughter of Jonas b Salomon Mendels (79) (2.9.1829-2.5.1902) & Grietje b Mozes de Groot (7.11.1839-aft 1902), in Amsterdam. Born on 19.12.1864 in Scheemda.

341. Rebecca b Juda Monnikendam. Born on 9.11.1869 in Brielle. died on 23.4.1943 in k Sobibor. Occupation: Verpleegster. She was never married.

342. Sientje b Juda Monnikendam. Born on 12.6.1873 in Brielle. died on 30.4.1943 in k Sobibor.

On 31.3.1895 when Sientje b Juda was 21, she first married Lodewijk b Michiel Leviticus in Amsterdam. Born on 15.2.1871 in Roermond. died on 29.10.1911 in Amsterdam?

son of Michiel Leviticus & Hendrina Fitt

They had the following children:
i. Max b Lodewijk Leviticus (13.5.1896-15.12.1942)
ii. Frits b Lodewijk Leviticus (23.8.1900-? )

Sientje b Juda second married Alfred b Liepman Bendien, son of Liepman b Salomon Bendien (29.12.1833-21.3.1906) & Henriette b NN Windmuller (1833-). Born on 13.8.1867 in Almelo-stad. died on 4.4.1929 in Arnhem.

343. Eva b Juda Monnikendam. Born on 12.11.1875 in Brielle. died on 11.6.1943 in k Sobibor.

On 5.4.1906 when Eva b Juda was 30, she married Isidore Henri b Hendrik Hartogensis, son of Hendrik b Jeronimus Hartogensis (19.11.1836-16.8.1902) & Ernestine b Isaac Levij (17.5.1850-12.1.1930), in Amsterdam. Born on 9.3.1876 in s-Hertogenbosch. died on 11.6.1943 in k Sobibor.
