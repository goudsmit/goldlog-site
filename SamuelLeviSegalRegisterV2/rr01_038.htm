Family of Philp b Simon Goudsmit (45) & Catharina b Jacob Goudsmit (50)

183. Simon b Philip Goudsmit. Born on 9.11.1844 in Oud-Beijerland. died on 28.5.1889 in Amsterdam. Occupation: Commissionair in Effecten, Winkelier in Garen, Band en Sajet, Koopman (1874-1889), Winkelier (1886).

15.5.1866 moving from Oud-Beijerland to Rotterdam; living Hoogstraat nr 33, in the house of his cousin Jonas Mozes Goudsmit.
16.4.1870 moving from Rotterdam to Amsterdam; starting a shop at Nieuwendijk nr 132 called “De Bijenkorf”
1874-1889 Living Amsterdam, Nieuwendijk 132
