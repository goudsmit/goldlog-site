Family of Kaatje/Lina b Mozes Goudsmit (77) & Louis b Marcus Gompertz

315. Mouritz b Louis Gompertz. Born on 17.9.1886 in Arnhem. died on 29.10.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

316. Marcus (Max) b Louis Gompertz. Born on 10.4.1889 in Arnhem. died on 4.11.1985 in Arnhem.

On 16.12.1914 when Marcus (Max) b Louis was 25, he married Charlotte b Benjamin Jacobs, daughter of Benjamin b Ravel Jacobs (13.1.1856-15.11.1933) & Henrietta b Marcus Gompertz (18.4.1853-19.7.1939), in Groningen. Born on 5.3.1890 in Groningen. died on 1.4.1978 in Arnhem.

317. Jacques b Louis Gompertz. Born on 12.11.1896 in Arnhem. died on 30.9.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim). Occupation: Arts (Doctor).

Jacques b Louis married Sara-Lina-Johanna b Jonas van Beem, daughter of Jonas b Heiman van Beem (25.11.1864-12.1.1943) & Johanna b Juda Gerzon (11.10.1874-11.6.1943). Born on 20.5.1902 in Haarlem. died on 30.9.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

They had the following children:
i. Johanna Charlotte b Jacques Gompertz (Died as Child) (17.5.1925-30.9.1942)
ii. Lodewijk Jack b Jacques Gompertz (Died as Child) (22.4.1928-2.3.1945)
