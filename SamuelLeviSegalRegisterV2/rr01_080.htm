Family of Heiman b Joël Wertheim (107) & Henriette b Gompfel Hochheimer

426. Julius b Heiman Wertheim. Born on 25.2.1879 in Bladel. died on 23.7.1879 in Bladel.

427. Jacob b Heiman Wertheim. Born on 29.4.1881 in Bladel. died on 22.2.1931 in Eindhoven. Occupation: Sigarenfabrikant, Makelaar in tabak.

On 7.10.1908 when Jacob b Heiman was 27, he married Hester b Elias Cohen, daughter of Elias b Emanuel Cohen (27.6.1845-19.7.1935) & Emilia b Hermann Heijmann (17.4.1858-), in s-Hertogenbosch. Born on 19.2.1883 in s-Hertogenbosch. died on 31.12.1944 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

They had the following children:
i. Emilie-Emma b Jacob Wertheim (2.8.1909-9.12.1943)
ii. Herman b Jacob Wertheim (17.2.1912-15.5.1944)
iii. Elias b Jacob Wertheim (11.3.1920-16.2.1945)

428. Clara b Heiman Wertheim. Born on 25.5.1883 in Bladel. died on 23.4.1943 in k Sobibor.

On 4.11.1908 when Clara b Heiman was 25, she married Simon b Joseph Trijbetz in Eindhoven, Strijp. Born on 27.5.1882 in Alkmaar. died on 23.4.1943 in k Sobibor.

son of Joseph Trijbetz (Trijbitz) & Marianne Bonn (Boom)
