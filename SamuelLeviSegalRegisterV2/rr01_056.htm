310. Rosa Johanna b Morits Gompertz. Born on 3.9.1893 in Arnhem. died on 10.9.1943 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

1942 living in Arnhem -- child survived the war

311. Charlotte-Helena b Morits Gompertz. Born on 27.5.1895 in Arnhem. died aft 1956.

On 29.3.1921 when Charlotte-Helena b Morits was 25, she married Nico b Juda Bolle, son of Juda-Maurits b Hartog Bolle (21.5.1865-4.6.1943) & Dina b Philip Abrahamson (13.8.1865-6.4.1930), in Arnhem. Born on 30.3.1896 in s-Gravenhage. died on 21.6.1956 in s-Gravenhage. Buried in Wassenaar, near s-Gravenhage. Occupation: Juwelier (1921).

They had the following children:
