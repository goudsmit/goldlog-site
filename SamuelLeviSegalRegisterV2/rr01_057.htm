Family of Jacques/Jacob b Mozes Goudsmit (76) & Louisa b Herodus de Jongh

312. Geertruida b Jacob Goudsmit. Born on 13.3.1886 in Rotterdam. died on 19.7.1912 in Hellendoorn. She was never married.

313. Maurits b Jacob Goudsmit. Born on 28.10.1890 in Rotterdam. died on 1.10.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim). Occupation: Meubelfabrikant (1920).

1920 Living in Utrecht

On 26.10.1920 when Maurits b Jacob was 29, he married Sophie-Marianne b Joseph van den Berg, daughter of Joseph b Abraham van den Berg (25.4.1866-8.5.1942) & Christina/Adele b Salomon Loeb (21.12.1860-21.8.1909), in s-Gravenhage. Born on 18.9.1894 in Leiden. died on 1.10.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

[NB: witnesses at marriage are Abraham Herschel Edersheim (age 62) and Moritz Loeb (age 56, uncle of the bride)]

They had the following children:
i. Tinie-Louisa-Flora b Maurits Goudsmit (31.8.1921-1.10.1942)
ii. Alfred b Jacob Goudsmit (Confidential, Male)
iii. Louisa-Maria-Anna b Maurits Goudsmit (Died as Child) (22.2.1931-1.10.1942)

314. Roza-Maria-Anna b Jacob Goudsmit. Born on 12.5.1901 in Rotterdam. died on 14.12.1979 in Utrecht. She was never married.
