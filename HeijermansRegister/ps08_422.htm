Notes for Marcus b Bernard Lissa
son of Bernard Lissa & Keetje Meijer -- named Mordechai ben Joël.
From 1809 till 1813 he was the acting Chief Rabbi of Rotterdam. In 1818 he was appointed as Chief Rabbi of the Region Brabant, but he fell ill and never arrived at his post in s-Hertogenbosch.
