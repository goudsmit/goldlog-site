Family of Philip b Jacob Rotterdam (van Gelder Sluijs) (11) & Rebecca b Levi van Dorth

25. Emanuel b Philip van Gelder Rotterdam. Born in 1735 in Amsterdam.

[NB: Emanuel’s grandfather Levi Salomon van Dorth is a brother of his wife’s father: Jacob Salomon van Dorth and his other grandfather Jacob Eliazar Sluijs is also his wife’s great-grandfather]

In 1765 Emanuel b Philip married Roosje b Jacob Sluijs van Dorth (28) , daughter of Jacob b Salomon van Dorth (abt 1690-) & Belia b Levinus Sluis (Sluijs) (12) (abt 1688-), in Amsterdam. Born in 1739 in Dordrecht. died on 3.6.1820 in Amsterdam.

They had one child:
43 i. Philip b Emanuel van Gelder Rotterdam (1773-26.11.1821)
