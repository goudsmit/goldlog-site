Family of Engeltje b Elkan Kosman (116) & Jacob b Mozes van Emden

187. Cornelia b Jacob van Emden. Born on 17.9.1884 in Rotterdam. died on 14.1.1943 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

On 28.11.1912 when Cornelia b Jacob was 28, she married Jacob Gerrit b Benjamin Wolf, son of Benjamin b Jacob Wolf (10.6.1854-) & Rosetta b Jacob Indemeij (10.7.1852-23.2.1943), in Rotterdam. Born on 31.1.1883 in Rotterdam.

They had the following children:
i. Roza Hetty b Jacob Wolf (4.5.1916-16.7.1943)
ii. Jacoba Bernardina b Jacob Wolf (28.12.1919-30.4.1943)

188. Mozes b Jacob van Emden. Born on 9.1.1886 in Rotterdam. died on 30.9.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

On 26.3.1919 when Mozes b Jacob was 33, he married Julie b Eduard Werij, daughter of Eduard b Anthony Werij (30.5.1854-14.2.1931) & Betje b Simon Swaab (17.1.1859-19.10.1942), in Rotterdam. Born on 13.4.1888 in Gouda. died on 16.8.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

They had one child:
i. Jacob b Mozes van Emden (2.4.1920-30.9.1942)
