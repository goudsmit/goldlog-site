48. Salomon b Marcus Sluis (Sluijs). Born on 9.7.1767 in Dordrecht. died on 6.6.1812 in Dordrecht.

1808 living Dordrecht: Kolfstraat, House C1610; married, 5 children

In 1796 Salomon b Marcus married Rebekka b David van Krevel (van Creefeld), daughter of David b Levi van Krevel (30.1.1742-2.11.1818) & Judith/Grietje b Philip (Philips) (abt 1745-9.3.1780), in Oud-Beijerland. Born in 1767 in Oud-Beijerland. died on 7.4.1853 in Dordrecht.

1812, as her husband dies, she remains with 7 children between 12 and 1 years old!]
1853, living Dordrecht, op den Hil (=Raamstraat), House D 512
