Family of Marianne b Salomon Rotterdam (Sluijs) (22) & Elias b Daniël (Daniëls, Nijmegen)

42. Jacob b Elias (Daniëls, Nijmegen). Born on 16.6.1734 in Amsterdam. died on 4.11.1811 in Amsterdam.

On 13.10.1782 when Jacob b Elias was 48, he married Fronica/Fromet b Mozes Polak (Engers), daughter of Mozes b Isaac Polak (Engers) (1717-) & Schoontje b Eliazar (Eliazar) (1715-), in s-Gravenhage. Born in 1756 in s-Gravenhage. died on 11.6.1833 in Amsterdam. Buried in Muiderberg.

7.1.1812 Amsterdam, NaamsAanneming (NameAdaption): “Daniëls” by Fromet Polak, widow of Jacob Elias Daniël; she has 2 sons and 2 daughters: Isaac (25), Mozes (20), Marianne (27) and Siena (20)

They had the following children:
54 i. Marianne b Jacob Daniëls (12.10.1783-4.10.1832)
55 ii. Elias b Jacob Daniëls (Twin) (29.5.1785-bef 1812)
56 iii. Isaac b Jacob Daniëls (Twin) (29.5.1785-aft 1812)
57 iv. Mozes b Jacob Daniëls (18.12.1791-aft 1816)
58 v. Schoontje/Siena b Jacob Daniëls (1792-23.10.1843)
