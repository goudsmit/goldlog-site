Eighth Generation (Continued)
Family of Eva b Mozes Goudsmit (107) & Lijpman b Benedictus de Vries

173. Albert b Lijpman de Vries. Born on 8.1.1866 in Amsterdam. died on 28.9.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim). He was never married.

Before 1940 he was working in Batavia, Nederlands Indie (= Djakarta, Indonesia) for the Bataafsche Petroleum Maatschappij (BPM).

174. Maurits b Lijpman de Vries. Born on 14.2.1867 in Amsterdam. died on 10.7.1945 in Indonesia, k Ambarawa. Buried in Indonesia, Kalibanteng, Semarang.

9.11.1886 moves to Rotterdam
later moving to Nederlands Indie (=Indonesia), working for the Bataafsche Petroleum Maatschappij (BPM)
he died in japanese Kamp (K7)

In 1903 Maurits b Lijpman married Cornélie Eerdmans in marriage by proxy. Born abt 1870 in Amsterdam?

They had the following children:
i. Evaline b Maurits de Vries (18.12.1903-)
ii. Leonard b Maurits de Vries (31.12.1904-abt 1964)
iii. Cornelia b Maurits de Vries (24.9.1906-13.2.1993)

175. Bertha b Lijpman de Vries. Born on 12.8.1868 in Amsterdam. died on 20.10.1872 in Amsterdam.

176. Antoon b Lijpman de Vries. Born on 4.2.1870 in Amsterdam. died on 26.9.1942 in k Central-Europe (missing).

177. John b Lijpman de Vries. Born on 19.5.1871 in Amsterdam. died on 1.10.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim). Occupation: Kantoorbediende (1913).

On 29.10.1913 when John b Lijpman was 42, he married Lea-Rebecca b Mozes Querido, daughter of Mozes b Daniël Querido (12.6.1863-11.2.1944) & Rebecca b David Casuto (8.4.1858-26.9.1934), in Amsterdam. Born on 2.9.1884 in Amsterdam. died on 1.10.1942 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim).

178. Johanna b Lijpman de Vries. Born on 14.8.1872 in Amsterdam. died on 25.1.1943 in k Auschwitz (Oswiecim). She was never married.

179. Annette b Lijpman de Vries. Born on 4.12.1874 in Amsterdam. died ? .

On 12.6.1901 when Annette b Lijpman was 26, she married Joseph b Abraham Stokvis in Amsterdam. Born abt 1875 in Amsterdam. died ? .
