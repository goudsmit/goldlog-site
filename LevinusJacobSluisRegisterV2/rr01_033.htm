108. Arnoldus b Meijer Noach. Born on 19.3.1833 in Dordrecht.

On 26.10.1864 when Arnoldus b Meijer was 31, he married Catharina b Samuel Heijmans, daughter of Samuel b Isaac Heijmans (5.1.1814-aft 1878) & Saartje b Elias Hartogs (Hartog) (5.10.1816-26.11.1871), in Dordrecht. Born on 9.8.1841 in Bergen op Zoom. Occupation: Pettenmaakster.

died nov 1911 Amsterdam (?)

109. Salomon b Meijer Noach. Born on 27.3.1835 in Dordrecht. died on 22.10.1911 in Arnhem. Occupation: Slager (1870).

1870 living in Arnhem

On 18.5.1870 when Salomon b Meijer was 35, he married Elsken b Abraham van Gelder, daughter of Abraham b Joseph van Gelder (1807-15.2.1892) & Mendel b Salomon Roodschild (1811-1.5.1884), in Zutphen. Born on 18.4.1843 in Zutphen. died on 15.6.1923 in Zutphen.

110. Levie b Meijer Noach (Meijer). Born on 13.5.1840 in Dordrecht. Occupation: Leerlooijer, Winkelier(1875).

1875 living in Dordrecht

On 17.8.1875 when Levie b Meijer was 35, he married Bertha b Cappel Cappel, daughter of Cappel b Salomon Cappel (1804-17.2.1851) & Sophia b NN Loewenthal (1805-30.5.1889), in Roermond. Born on 8.7.1847 in Germany, Brüggen. Occupation: Kraamster (1875).

[NB: in the Marriage Attachments there are two german documents: #1 stating her birth as daughter of Coppel Coppel and Sophia Loewenthal and #2 stating the death of her father Cappel Cappel son of Salomon Cappel and Sibilla Cappel, spouse of Sophia Löwenthal -- Witness at her marriage is a.o. her uncle Samuel Cappel, age 61, slagter living in Roermond -- both signatures in the marriage act read without doubt: Cappel and not Coppel]
[NB: It is very probable that her father is a brother of Isac Salomon Cappel (Kappel) who was married to Catharina Samuel Goudsmit, sister of Hertog Samuel Goudsmit in Roermond, whose profession was Leerlooijer (Tanner), like Levie Noach]

They had one child:
183 i. Johanna b Levi Noach (1884-)
