First Generation
1. Jacob b Eliazar (Sluijs). Born abt 1635 in Germany, Kleve region. died on 7.4.1702 in Rotterdam. Occupation: Tabakshandelaar.

called Jokele -- also stated as Jacob Elias -- son of Elyakim/Getshlik Halevi
[NB: several data about the Sluis family thanks to Piet Sanders1]
[NB: He was married three times and had many children -- about several of them isn’t known much so far, not about their order of birth, not who was their mother]
1674 Living Rotterdam
1699 Rotterdam, his house at Specerije Vaart also called Delftsevaart, eastside.

Jacob b Eliazar first married Dina b Levi (Levie). Born abt 1635 in Amsterdam? died on 30.11.1678 in Rotterdam.

They had the following children:
2 i. Levinus/Levi b Jacob Sluis (Sluijs) (abt 1665-aft 1746)
3 ii. Abigael b Jacob (Sluijs) (abt 1670-)

Jacob b Eliazar second married Sara b Tobias Magnus. Born abt 1650 in Amsterdam. died on 20.10.1690 in Rotterdam.

daughter of Tobias Magnus (from Moras) & Sebilla Salomons (from Hanau)

They had the following children:
4 i. Eliazar b Jacob Sluijs (van der Sluis) (abt 1680-bef 1726)
5 ii. Mozes b Jacob (Sluijs) (abt 1680-)
6 iii. Salomon b Jacob Rotterdam (Sluijs) (abt 1685-31.12.1751)
7 iv. Emanuel b Jacob (Sluijs) (abt 1685-)
8 v. Jonas b Jacob (Sluijs) (abt 1685-bef 1726)
9 vi. Joseph b Jacob (Sluijs) (abt 1685-bef 1726)
10 vii. Hartog b Jacob (Sluijs) (abt 1685-)

Jacob b Eliazar third married Sara b Levi? (Levie). Born abt 1665 in Rotterdam? died aft 1727 in Amsterdam?

They had one child:
11 i. Philip b Jacob Rotterdam (van Gelder Sluijs) (1698-)
