Family of Judic b Salomon Sluis (Sluijs) (35) & Corstiaan b Barend (Kosman)

50. Samuel b Corstiaan Barend Kosman. Born on 9.3.1786 in Dordrecht. died on 16.3.1870 in Rotterdam.

son of Barend Kosman (should be: Kosman Barend) & Judik Salomon Sluis (1825: both parents living in Dordrecht)
[NB: confusion about his birthdate: 23.11.1784 Dordrecht (Liste Civique) -- circumcision 16.2.1786 Rotterdam (Besnijdenisregister) -- 9.3.1786 Dordrecht (Military Certificate)]

On 3.8.1805 when Samuel b Corstiaan Barend was 19, he first married Leentje b Elkan (Elkan) in Dordrecht. Born abt 1785 in Amsterdam. died on 24.5.1824 in Dordrecht.

daughter of Elkan Barend & Eva Levij
1805 living Dordrecht, Mariënbornstraat

They had the following children:
80 i. Elkan b Samuel Kosman (11.9.1806-9.9.1879)
81 ii. Barend b Samuel Kosman (6.4.1808-19.8.1886)
82 iii. Levi b Samuel Kosman (14.2.1810-)
83 iv. Salomon b Samuel Kosman (1817-12.7.1900)

On 16.3.1825 when Samuel b Corstiaan Barend was 39, he second married Sara b Levi Zoest in Rotterdam. Born on 15.9.1792 in Rotterdam.

daughter of Levy Samuel Zoest (d.12.5.1814 Rotterdam) & Neeltje Abraham van Engelen
