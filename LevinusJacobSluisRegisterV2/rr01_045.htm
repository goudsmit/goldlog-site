Family of Rebecca b Mozes Goudsmit (101) & Hertz b Abraham van Gelder

154. Abraham b Hertz van Gelder. Born on 20.6.1850 in Schoonhoven. died on 29.4.1914 in Gouda. Occupation: Koopman.

1880 living House B 417a (= Oostsingel)
1883 moving to Koestraat 143
1889 moving to Amsterdam

On 25.7.1877 when Abraham b Hertz was 27, he married Aleida b Benjamin Gelder, daughter of Benjamin b Jacob Gelder (15.6.1810-28.3.1888) & Carolina/Cornelia b Calmer Norden (22.3.1813-27.2.1876), in Meppel. Born on 10.5.1852 in Meppel. died on 30.1.1930 in Hilversum. They were divorced on 23.9.1897 in Amsterdam.

also spelled Alijda

They had the following children:
i. Cornelia/Carolina Rebecca b Abraham van Gelder (29.7.1878-3.9.1943)
ii. Rosalie Rebecca b Abraham van Gelder (Died as Infant) (21.8.1879-22.2.1880)
iii. Antoinette Rebecca b Abraham van Gelder (21.11.1883-3.9.1943)
iv. Rebecca b Abraham van Gelder (Died as Child) (24.12.1884-14.11.1888)

155. daughter of Hertz van Gelder. Born on 13.10.1851 in Schoonhoven. died on 13.10.1851 in Schoonhoven.

156. Belia b Hertz van Gelder. Born on 22.5.1856 in Schoonhoven. died on 24.5.1856 in Schoonhoven.
