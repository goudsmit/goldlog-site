184. Abraham b Mozes Meijer. Born in 1887 in Dordrecht.

On 31.7.1918 Abraham b Mozes married Heintje b Jacob Hartogs, daughter of Jacob b David Hartogs (15.9.1858-21.3.1932) & Betje b Jacob Polak (11.4.1862-14.2.1917), in Zaandam. Born on 22.7.1891 in Amsterdam. died on 2.7.1943 in k Sobibor.
