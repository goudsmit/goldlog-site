Fourth Generation
Family of Belia b Levinus Sluis (Sluijs) (12) & Jacob b Salomon van Dorth

26. Isaac b Jacob van Dorth. Born abt 1730 in Dordrecht.

[NB: in mariage act stated as Isaek Salomons Dort Jacquesz]
1761 living Dordrecht, Heer Heijmansuijsstraat

On 9.1.1761 Isaac b Jacob married Clara b Salomon Sluis (Sluijs) (34) , daughter of Salomon b Levinus Sluis (Sluijs) (15) (abt 1700-) & Adriana b Nathan Wolf Cohen (abt 1700-), in Dordrecht. Born abt 1735 in Dordrecht.

1761 living Dordrecht, Kromme Elleboog

27. Abigael b Jacob van Dorth. Born in 1738 in Dordrecht.

stated as Abigael Jacob Salomon

In 1773 Abigael b Jacob married Wolf b Simon Stommes in Amsterdam. Born in 1733 in Poland, Breslau. died on 8.7.1793 in Amsterdam. Buried in Muiderberg.

He was earlier married to Anna Samson

28. Roosje b Jacob Sluijs van Dorth. Born in 1739 in Dordrecht. died on 3.6.1820 in Amsterdam.

In 1765 Roosje b Jacob married Emanuel b Philip van Gelder Rotterdam (25) , son of Philip b Jacob Rotterdam (van Gelder Sluijs) (11) (1698-) & Rebecca b Levi van Dorth (1699-), in Amsterdam. Born in 1735 in Amsterdam.

[NB: Emanuel’s grandfather Levi Salomon van Dorth is a brother of his wife’s father: Jacob Salomon van Dorth and his other grandfather Jacob Eliazar Sluijs is also his wife’s great-grandfather]

They had one child:
43 i. Philip b Emanuel van Gelder Rotterdam (1773-26.11.1821)
