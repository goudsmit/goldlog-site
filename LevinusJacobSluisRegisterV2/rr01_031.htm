99. daughter b Samuel Wiener (Benjamin Wiener). Born on 11.8.1824 in Oud-Beijerland. died on 11.8.1824 in Oud-Beijerland.

100. Jacob b Samuel Wiener (Benjamin Wiener). Born on 12.8.1825 in Oud-Beijerland. died on 11.8.1890 in Renkum. Occupation: Fabrikant.

When Jacob was 8 month old his father died. He became the (step) son of his mothers second spouse, Mozes Jonas Goudsmit, who also was a cousin of his father (= nephew of his grandmother Rebecca).

On 5.1.1848 when Jacob b Samuel was 22, he first married Maartje-Sally b Salomon van Witsen, daughter of Salomon b Isaak van Witsen (van Witzen) (15.9.1798-26.10.1830) & Betje b Emanuel Simons van Noorden (10.1.1800-aft 1855), in s-Gravenhage. Born on 30.7.1822 in s-Gravenhage. died on 27.11.1870 in Rotterdam.

Maartje’s mother Betje was a sister of Rachel Emanuel Simons van Noorden, mother of Mietje Eliazar Denekamp, who was married to Jonas Mozes Goudsmit, the oldest (step)brother of Maartje’s spouse Jacob Wiener.
