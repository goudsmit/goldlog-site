Family of Meijer b Joseph Cohen de Heer (92) & Rosetta b Philip (Cohen) de Heer

124. Joseph b Meijer Cohen (de Heer). Born on 19.6.1871 in Dordrecht. died on 20.3.1931 in Dordrecht.

On 15.11.1896 when Joseph b Meijer was 25, he married Bruintje b Levi van Geuns, daughter of Levi b Eliazar van Geuns (14.2.1824-21.6.1919) & Hanna b Betje Polak (7.6.1831-29.4.1913), in Groningen. Born on 5.9.1864 in Groningen. died on 21.12.1933 in Dordrecht.

125. Philip b Meijer Cohen. Born on 29.7.1873 in Dordrecht.

On 27.9.1895 when Philip b Meijer was 22, he married Mina b Jacob Eijsman (Eismann), daughter of Jacob b Mozes Eijsman (Eismann) (25.5.1845-) & Hanchen b Nathan Stein (1844-20.5.1903), in s-Hertogenbosch. Born on 16.2.1871 in Venlo.

126. Sophia b Meijer Cohen (de Heer). Born on 1.8.1875 in Dordrecht. died on 9.12.1925 in Klundert.

[NB: acc to his death act he was married to Leentje Mozes]
