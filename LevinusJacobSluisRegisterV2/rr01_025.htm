67. Belia b Salomon Sluis (Sluijs). Born on 13.12.1799 in Dordrecht. died on 28.1.1845 in Oud-Beijerland. Occupation: Dienstmeid (1823), Koopvrouw (1827).

1899 born in Dordrecht, in de Raamstraat (acc to Notoriety act)
1823 living in Oud-Beijerland

On 30.11.1823 when Belia b Salomon was 23, she first married Samuel b Jacob Wiener (Benjamin Wiener), son of Jacob b Menachem Wiener (Benjamin Wiener) (1750-21.8.1822) & Rebecca b Samuel Levi (Goldschmidt) (abt 1750-4.9.1810), in Oud-Beijerland. Born on 4.4.1788 in Bergeijk. died on 11.4.1826 in Oud-Beijerland. Occupation: Koopman (1823).

They had the following children:
99 i. daughter b Samuel Wiener (Benjamin Wiener) (Stillborn) (11.8.1824-11.8.1824)
100 ii. Jacob b Samuel Wiener (Benjamin Wiener) (12.8.1825-11.8.1890)

On 27.5.1827 when Belia b Salomon was 27, she second married Mozes b Jonas Goudsmit, son of Jonas b Samuel Goudsmit (Levi Segal Goldschmidt) (4.5.1739-18.2.1817) & Schoontje b Meijer Frank (10.9.1756-6.3.1843), in Oud-Beijerland. Born on 22.6.1800 in Belgium, Lommel. died on 24.12.1883 in Rotterdam. Occupation: Koopman (1827, 1845), Winkelier, Houder Bank van Lening, Mede-oprichter Synagoges Oud-Beijerland en Brielle.

1812 moving to Oud-Beijerland, house nr 358, Molendijk
1825 living in Oud-Beijerland, house nr 222, Westvoorstraat
1839 living in Oud-Beijerland, house nr 14, Oostvoorstraat, later Lange Voorstraat
1846 moving to Brielle, Voorstraat 43 (= today nr 8)
1861 moving to Brielle, Voorstraat 76 (= today nr 93)
15.5.1868 moving to Rotterdam, Steiger 32 (was wijk 7, huis 139)
